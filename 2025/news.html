Nvidia sky rockets

Nvidia’s stock soared to a record $155 in June 2025, driven by its leadership in AI chips and eased U.S.-China trade tensions. The company’s partnerships and new AI products boosted investor confidence, pushing its market value past $3.7 trillion. Analysts predict Nvidia could soon hit $4 trillion. Despite insiders selling over $1 billion in shares, Nvidia remains a top AI stock with strong growth potential.
